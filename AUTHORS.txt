History & Authorship of BSMAPz:


BSMAPz is an updated and optimized version of BSMAP.

BSMAPz was written by Greg Zynda, and adapted from BSMAP, which
was written by Yuanxin Xi and Wei Li.


If you make use of the this package, please credit the authors
as follows:

Please acknowledge use of this program by name.

Please also cite the original BSMAP paper:
	Yuanxin Xi and Wei Li, "BSMAP: whole genome bisulfite
sequence MAPping program" (2009) BMC Bioinformatics 2009, 10:232
